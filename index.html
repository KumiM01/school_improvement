DaKota Powerhouse Health Foods Program
School Improvement Program

1. Afternoon Class Teacher Program:

Implementation:

A stipend sourced from 'DaKota Project' revenue will be allocated to hiring retired teachers or unemployed graduates as afternoon teachers.

Methodology:

  1. Daily recording forms (either paper or digital) will be provided to teachers, using student numbers for anonymity.
  2. A local youth team in the school's computer lab will be responsible for inputting and organizing the data, as well as maintaining the schools’ website.
  3. The recorded information will be shared with afternoon teachers, with a specific focus on the day’s weaker students and subjects.

2. IT Education Program:

Implementation:

'DaKota Project' funds will be utilized to train and employ youth for IT education.

Methodology:

  1. Local youth will undergo training in IT skills.
  2. They will then be tasked with teaching all students and teachers, creating a tech-savvy school environment.

Expected Outcomes:

Budget Allocation:

A percentage of 'DaKota Project' revenue will be allocated to stipends, training, and salaries for afternoon teachers and IT education.
